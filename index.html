Sara Ibarra

About Me

Hello! My name is Sara Ibarra. I grew up in a small town in Arizona, where most people stay forever. I was always the one that wanted to experience more and move beyond what was comfortable. I moved to Colorado 7 years ago and fell in love with the state. I worked a few places that were stepping stones for what was to come. I finally found a position with Ford Credit in July 2019 and felt like this was the career for me.

When I am not at work, I enjoy spending time watching football (Go Cardinals!), kayaking, hiking, coaching, and making some sort of craft. One of the ways life remains full, exciting, healthy for me is to create. One of my favorite things to do is to take something that was going to be thrown out and turn it into something new. I have always been a self-learner and have become good friends with YouTube and Pinterest. I learned how to make everything from a cheesecake to a side table. My desire is to grow in the freedom to continue taking my own thoughts, ideas, designs, and turning them into reality, so that one day others are using my Pinterest and YouTube channels as their examples.

The Why

Entering into the field of software engineering provides me with endless opportunities to create. As I said previously, what motivates me in life is having an outlet to express myself. This marries perfectly with Ford's future goals. Marion Harris has said, "We need to start thinking of ourselves as a software company more than a finance company." I want to be part of Ford Credit for many years to come. I want to grow with the company and be a part of the transformation from thinking like a finance company to becoming a software company.

After these last six weeks, I discovered that part of being a software engineer involves constant problem-solving, research, and creativity to identify the code that needs correction in order to bring the project to life. This is very similar to my learning process, as outlined under "My Passion". As I progressed through the project, I came to realize that this kind of work excites me and motivates me to reach the finish line. I look forward to using my creativity to help Ford evolve in the field of technology!
